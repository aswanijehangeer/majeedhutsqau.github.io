About Majeed Huts

Majeed Huts is one of oldest cafe for Quaid e Azam University, Islamabad. It offers a variety of traditional foods. Graduates occasionally visit the place to remember their student Life memories and enjoy the same taste at cafe.

Majeed Huts was founded in 1970. Majeed Huts was built from a love of Crisis-Omelet. We hope Majeed Huts adds a unique and interesting place to our customers.

Majeed Huts Famous Dish

What is the most famous dish at Majeed Huts?

Crisis-Omelet, a small spoon of each item that is left, whatever curry or even soup it may be, is all stir fried up, then placed into the center of a slight overly oily (intentional) omelet. And the new dish is born a Crisis omelet.

Chicken Laziza, large chunks of chicken breast and peppery masala spices successfully combine to make this one of the most-known dishes from this entire part of the world. It is rich and creamy, and packed full of flavor.
